MUSEUM/OF/CANDY

Step into a world of sweetness and wonder as you enter the Museum of Candy. Our mission is to celebrate the history, artistry, and sheer joy of candy-making throughout the ages. From ancient confections to cutting-edge treats, we've curated a one-of-a-kind experience that will tickle your taste buds and ignite your imagination.

MUSEUM OF CANDY

Take a stroll down memory lane as you explore our exhibit on the evolution of candy. Discover how the earliest civilizations indulged their sweet cravings and learn how candy has played a role in cultures around the world. From honeyed delights of antiquity to the high-tech sweets of today, our exhibits offer a glimpse into the rich history of sugary treasures.

MUSEUM OF CANDY

Ever wondered how your favorite candies are made? Dive into the fascinating world of candy science at our interactive displays. Learn about the chemistry behind crystallization, the physics of chocolate tempering, and the art of flavor creation. You might even get a chance to witness a live candy-making demonstration!

MUSEUM OF CANDY

Step into our enchanting Candy Wonderland, a whimsical space filled with larger-than-life candy sculptures and installations. Explore a life-sized gingerbread house, stroll through a candy cane forest, and marvel at a colossal chocolate waterfall. It's a dream come true for candy enthusiasts of all ages.
